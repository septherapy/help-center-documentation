---
title: Admitting students
---
Students who are requesting to be admitted to the session will be placed in your waiting room. They will be able to check their devices and enter their name before confirming they are ready for the session. Once a student has entered your waiting room, you will be notified that a new participant is requesting access.

If you are outside your therapy room, the notification will prompt you to manage the request in the therapy room. Waiting participants will be listed on your dashboard. 

If you are already in the therapy room, you can manage the request in the participants panel. Students requesting access will show up in the waiting room section of the panel.

When students first enter your waiting room, they will be identified as "New participant". This label will update to their name once they have entered one. You will also be able to see whether their camera and microphone are functioning properly and if they currently have their camera off or microphone muted. 

Once a student confirms they are ready for the session, you will be able to decline or admit them.

If you decline entry, the student will be sent back to the waiting room though they can request to be admitted again.

If you admit the student, you will be presented with a list of student records to link to the student. Student records allow the platform to log the start and end time of the session to record the duration. If a student record does not exist for the student, you may create a new one by entering the their name, date of birth, and selecting the school they attend (see [Student records](/providers/student-records) for more details). 

Once you have selected an existing student record or created a new one, click on **Confirm** to complete the process. The student's name will now show up in the current session section in the participants panel and their primary camera video display box will become visible.
